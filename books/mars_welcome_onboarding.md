# Welcome to Mars!

## Mars Colonial Authority · Personnel Division

You have been cleared for surface assignment. This booklet is your authoritative onboarding guide as a **Generation II incomer** — para-military settlers extending the work begun by the First Generation landers, who reached Mars and **did not return**.

![Valles habitat cluster at dusk](images/mars-habitat.svg)

### A message from the Director of Settlement

On behalf of the Mars Colonial Authority, welcome to the red frontier. You were selected not for comfort, but for **composure under constraint**, **discipline without fanaticism**, and an unambiguous commitment to **mission success and species survival**.

Every incomer in your cohort has completed full psychological and behavioral screening. You are certified **free of extremist ideologies and destabilizing conduct**. What remains is voluntary, life-long dedication to a colony that must endure without Earth’s daily reinforcement.

Read this document completely before your first extravehicular orientation. Initial questions route through your squad liaison; doctrine questions route through Chaplain-Counselor Services.

## Who we are — Generation II

### Lineage and purpose

- **First Generation** — Pioneer landers who established the surface beachhead; perished in service
- **Generation II** — You: follow-on settlers, pre-screened, para-military in structure
- **MCA** — Mars Colonial Authority: civil-military governing corporation

Generation II does not replace the First Generation. We **inherit their beachhead** and convert sacrifice into permanence. Your training emphasizes:

1. **Collective survival** over individual acclaim
2. **Measured force** — de-escalation first, defense when required
3. **Transparent chain of command** with moral accountability
4. **Stewardship** of air, water, soil analogues, and human trust

::: box Mission Statement
**Extend human civilization to Mars; preserve the species; honor the fallen; refuse despair.**

We are settlers under contract and under covenant — with one another, with the MCA, and with the principles that preceded us on Earth.
:::

## Pre-screening and conduct standards

All incomers pass MCA Panel 14 clearance:

- **Behavioral stability** under isolation, sleep compression, and high-consequence tasks
- **Absence of extremist affiliation** or agitation rhetoric (political, religious, or ideological)
- **Willingness for life-commitment** to colonial service, with informed consent documented
- **Team interoperability** across nationality, creed, and specialty

Para-military structure means **civilian law plus field discipline**: you wear no national uniform, but you accept ranked coordination during emergencies, EVA, and habitat breach protocols.

**Zero tolerance:** sabotage, hoarding of life-support assets, coercion, or unauthorized separatist organizing. Report concerns through the Ethics Hotline (Channel 7).

## Founding principles on a new world

![In God We Trust — founding seal](images/mars-oath.svg)

We do not leave humanity’s moral vocabulary behind. The MCA explicitly affirms **In God We Trust** as a founding principle — not as sectarian test, but as recognition that **conscience, humility, and sacred duty** anchor lawful society.

### Civic inheritance we carry forward

- **Rule of law** before rule of personality
- **Freedom of worship** within safety and mutual respect
- **Due process** even under resource stress
- **Truthful record-keeping** — the colony survives on honest telemetry
- **Perseverance** — *novus ordo laboris*, not despair

::: briefing Chaplain-Counselor note
Weekly voluntary services are available in Hab Commons B. Attendance is never scored. **Life-commitment** is to mission and species survival — not to the abandonment of faith, family memory, or moral restraint.
:::

## Memorial — First Generation landers

![Memorial to the First Generation](images/mars-memorial.svg)

The First Generation completed seventeen successful surface landings before cumulative habitat loss ended their era. Their names are inscribed on the Valles Memorial Ring. Each Generation II squad rotates guard duty there during solstice week.

When you pass the Memorial Ring, you are expected to pause. Silence is sufficient. Optional phrase, spoken or unspoken:

::: box Memorial phrase
*They reached. We remain.*
:::

Do not romanticize their deaths. **Learn from their telemetry.** Your onboarding includes mandatory review of First Generation failure modes: pressure cascade, dust intrusion, and command latency.

## Your first seven sols

### Sol 0–1: Arrival and quarantine

- Medical baseline and neurocognitive check
- Issue of personal dosimeter and comm token
- Assignment to squad, bunk, and work rotation

### Sol 2–3: Habitat orientation

- Airlock drills (graded)
- Hydroponics walk-through
- Introduction to MCA Code of Settlement Conduct

### Sol 4–5: Field skills

- EVA buddy system certification
- Rover path rules and lost-line procedures
- Emergency rally points Alpha through Delta

### Sol 6–7: Integration

- Mentor pairing with a Generation II senior (minimum one Martian year tenure)
- Signed **Life-Commitment Acknowledgment** (LCA-2)
- First community meal in Hab Commons — attendance required

## Life-commitment acknowledgment

By signing LCA-2, you affirm:

1. Understanding that **return to Earth is not guaranteed** and may be unavailable for the duration of service
2. Acceptance of **para-military coordination** during declared contingencies
3. Commitment to **species survival** as the overriding success metric of the colony
4. Ongoing adherence to **MCA conduct standards** and founding principles, including **In God We Trust** as corporate moral anchor

This is not indenture. This is **informed covenant**. Counseling withdrawal windows exist before Sol 10 only; afterward, separation is governed by MCA Articles 12–14.

::: box Ethics reminder
Strength without restraint is a breach risk. The MCA rejects cruelty, humiliation, and ideological purges. **Discipline serves survival — never vanity.**
:::

Welcome to Mars. Work with honor. Trust with care. Endure with purpose.
